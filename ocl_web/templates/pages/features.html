Lower costs by getting rid of unnecessary local terminology servers. OCL provides access to all major terminologies, such as SNOMED CT, ICD version 9 and 10, CPT, and more. One source for terminology up to indicators.

Indicators, clinical quality measures, and terminology can be managed all in one place.

Collaborative from the ground up.

Open Concept Lab is the first cloud-based global collaborative project to make the benefit of health information standards available to health systems and implementers everywhere. Collaboration within projects and across the organizations and countries is essential to reducing duplicative efforts, lowering costs, and improving health for patients all over the world.

Preferentially focused on the developing world.

Terminology standards benefit health systems globally, but there is an accute need to lower barriers to adopting health informatics best practices in the low- and middle-income countries.
